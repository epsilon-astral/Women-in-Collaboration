Empowering Women Across Every Sector

At Women in Collaboration (WiC), we believe that entrepreneurship has no boundaries—and neither should the support available to women pursuing their business dreams. Whether you are launching a startup, expanding an existing venture, or innovating in a niche field, WiC is here to empower you with tailored resources, mentorship, and opportunities across all industries.

Real Estate & Construction

We support women in property investment, real estate development, and construction management, offering networking opportunities with industry leaders and investors to scale their businesses.

Technology & Innovation

From tech startups to AI-driven solutions, we provide mentorship, funding connections, and digital skill-building programs to help women break into the highly competitive tech landscape.



Any Industry, Any Business

No matter the field, WiC is committed to providing women entrepreneurs with the knowledge, funding, and mentorship needed to thrive. Our inclusive programs ensure that every woman, in every industry, has the tools to succeed.

Fashion, Beauty & Lifestyle

Entrepreneurs in fashion, cosmetics, wellness, and e-commerce can access expert-led training on branding, retail strategies, digital marketing, and supply chain management to grow their brands successfully.

Media & Entertainment

From content creators to filmmakers, we help women entrepreneurs build strong media brands, navigate production funding, and connect with industry professionals to break barriers in entertainment.

Food & Hospitality

For women in catering, restaurants, hospitality, and food production, WiC offers business development programs on menu innovation, financial planning, restaurant management, and food distribution strategies.

Finance & Consulting

Women entering financial services, accounting, and business consulting gain access to high-level training, investment opportunities, and mentorship from financial experts to establish credibility and expertise in the field.

Community

Build a Powerful Network
Entrepreneurship can feel lonely, but with WiC, you’ll never have to do it alone! Meet like-minded women who share your passion, struggles, and ambitions. Whether you're looking for a business partner, mentor, or industry connections, our community is here to support you.


Access Exclusive Resources & Support
As a member, you gain access to:
Funding & Investment Opportunities – Discover grants, investors, and sponsorships to grow your business.
Business Toolkits & Training – Get templates, courses, and expert guidance tailored to your industry.
Live Events & Webinars – Learn from top women entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and business coaches.
One-on-One Mentorship – Connect with experienced mentors who can help guide you through challenges.


Collaborate & Grow Together
WiC isn’t just a network—it’s a movement! Whether you need co-founders, team members, or simply inspiration, our community fosters real collaborations that turn ideas into reality.
💡 Did you know? 80% of women entrepreneurs who actively network and collaborate are more likely to succeed than those who work alone.
